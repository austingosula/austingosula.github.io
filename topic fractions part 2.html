Simplest form of a fraction-

If a fraction is of the form a/b in which a and b have no common factor other than 1 and b not equal to 0 then the fraction is said to be in its simplest or lowest form.

example what fraction of an hour is 21 minutes? solution-

one hour=60 minutes

then,21 minutes of an hour =21/60(divide the term by 3 as it is divisible by 3 only) now you get 7/20.
